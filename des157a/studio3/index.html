GAME OF DOG vs CAT

Dog and Cat face off! Each pet rolls the dice (paws?). The value increases your score, unless either paw is a "one". Then it's the other pet's turn. Otherwise, the current pet can roll again or pass. First to get 30 points or higher wins. Psst! Watch out for the snakes. Two one's reduces your score to 0.
